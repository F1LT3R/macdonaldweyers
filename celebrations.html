Around The World

As was previously mentioned (see 'About Us') our hero's families are spread across the globe. In fact, much like the heyday of the British empire, the sun never sets on the extended MacDonald-Weyers clan.*

As wonderful as this is, it also means a bit of a logistical puzzle trying to get all of the families and friends in one place at one time. And since you're reading this, that means one of these wonderful events will be just for you!

Wedding Ceremony

at twelve o'clock, EST

Please join in tuning in for the live wedding broadcast on February 7th at noon EST. The video will be hosted on our church's live web-cast page, parkstreet.org/live.
